The Beginning

More Than
A Garment

ITS DANNY began not in a studio, but in the vision of a man determined to elevate African fashion, a vision built on conviction, craftsmanship, and the belief that African style deserves to stand proudly on the global stage, allowing every man to walk into any room with confidence and distinction.

What started as a determined pursuit to refine African menswear quickly grew into something greater. Word spread. Men began seeking pieces that reflected confidence and distinction. Grooms came for their wedding Agbadas. Professionals came for their Senator sets. Every client carried a vision, and every garment we crafted became a statement of it.

"We don't just make clothes. We make the garment a man reaches for when the moment matters most."

Today, ITS DANNY serves clients across Nigeria and the diaspora — offering fully custom-tailored African menswear, from measurement to delivery. But the spirit hasn't changed: every piece is personal, every stitch is intentional, and every customer walks away dressed like the man he knows himself to be.

What We Stand For

Our
Values

🧵
Craft Above All

We refuse shortcuts. Every garment is cut by experienced hands, stitched with precision, and finished with the kind of attention that only comes from genuine pride in the work.

🌍
Culture, Always

African fashion is not a trend — it is a living tradition. We draw from Yoruba, Igbo, Hausa, and pan-African textile heritage, translating ancient symbols into garments built for today's man.

📐
Perfect Fit, Every Time

Off-the-rack was never our language. We take every measurement by hand and tailor every garment to the individual — because a well-fitted piece changes how a man moves and carries himself.

👑
For Fathers & Sons

Some of our most meaningful work is the matching pieces we create for fathers and their boys. We believe style is a language passed between generations.

🤝
Client Relationship First

We know your name. We remember what you ordered for your wedding. ITS DANNY isn't a transactional brand — it is a long-term relationship between a tailor and the men he dresses.

⚡
Speed Without Sacrifice

We deliver most garments within 5–7 days of measurement confirmation, and we include a complimentary alteration on every piece — because the job isn't done until the fit is right.

The Founder

A Tailor Who
Believes in
Identity

"I grew up watching my father get dressed for Sunday service. The way he put on his Agbada — slowly, deliberately — told me everything I needed to know about what clothes can mean."

Danny trained under master tailors in Lagos for years before opening his own studio. He wanted to create something specific: a space where African men could walk in with a vision and walk out with a garment that exceeded it.

His approach is hands-on at every stage. From selecting fabrics with clients to supervising every measurement and fitting, Danny has never stepped away from the craft.

Beyond the studio, Danny is passionate about preserving African textile traditions — sourcing authentic Aso-Oke, Adire, and Ankara directly from artisans who carry generational knowledge in their hands.

Our Journey

Built Over
Years of
Craft

2019
The First Stitch

ITS DANNY began as a small personal tailoring practice — serving friends, family, and neighbours in Lagos. The first pieces were Agbadas and Senator sets made for weddings and naming ceremonies.

2020
The Boys Collection Is Born

Fathers began requesting matching outfits for their sons. Danny launched a dedicated boys' line — miniature versions of the adult catalogue, built with the same precision and care.

2021
The Studio Opens

ITS DANNY moved from home-based operations into a dedicated studio in Lagos. A small team of trained tailors joined, allowing the brand to scale while maintaining handcrafted quality.

2023
Serving the Diaspora

With the launch of our remote measurement guide, ITS DANNY began serving Nigerian men in the UK, US, Canada, and beyond. Clients book online and have custom pieces shipped directly to their door.

2026
2,400 Pieces. Still Counting.

Today, ITS DANNY has clothed over 2,400 men and boys across Nigeria and the diaspora. The mission remains unchanged: African men, dressed with pride.
